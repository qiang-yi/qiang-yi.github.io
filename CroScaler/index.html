Welcome to CroScalar

This project overcomes the challenges of multi-scale spatiotemporal analysis by rearranging space, time, and scale in a unified modeling framework. Software tools are being developed within this framework to detect anomalies, to model relationships, and to monitor uncertainty propagation across space and time, at single and linked spatiotemporal resolutions. The tools and self-paced tutorials will be made available in the public domain.
